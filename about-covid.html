Coronavirus

Coronavirus also known as Covid-19 is a pnuemonia-like disease that affects respiratory system of human body.

It started in Chinese City of Wuhan late december 2019 and spread to the rest of the world at alarming rate.

Symptoms

Covid-19 affects different in different ways.most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalizatiion.

Most common Symptoms:
  • Fever
  • Dry cough
  • Tiredness
Less common symptoms:
  • Headache
  • Sore throat
  • Diarrhoea
  • Loss of taste and smell
  • Conjunctivitis
Seroius symptoms:
  • Difficulty in breathing or shortness of breath
  • Chest pain or pressure
  • loss of speech or movement

Prevention

Avoid handshakes
Wash hands with clean water nd soap
Use sanitizer
Wear face mask
Do not touch your nose
Do not touch your mouth
Do not touch your eyes
Avoid smoking
Cough or sneeze in your elbow
Social distance
